Analytics

  • www.freecounterstat.com — Dy befault, blocked by uBlock Origin on the international domain, but several national domains are not, like www.contatoreaccessi.com (IT).
    • Multiple .ovh subdomains handle the image serving and the access counting, they appear to be functionally equivalent and swappable in case one is blocked, but the already counted accesses are bound to each specific domain, aka you can't swap the domain you embed while keeping the already counted accesses.
  • GoatCounter — Easy analytics. Open-source, self-hostable, GDPR-compliant. No tracking of personal data, and works without JS — [Git]
    • Note: GoatCounter tracking endpoints are blocked by the default uBlock Origin configuration, must selfhost or arrange some kind of proxy to bypass it.
  • Moe Counter! (count.getloli.com)
  • Start Hits — 🚀 Easy way to know how many visitors are viewing your Github, Website, Notion 🎉 — [Git]
